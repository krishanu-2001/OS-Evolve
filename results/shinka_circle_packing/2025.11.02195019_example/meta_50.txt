2. **Occasional Multi-Circle Moves During Annealing**  
   Modify the simulated annealing phase to occasionally (e.g., every 50–100 iterations) propose coordinated moves involving two or more nearby circles, rather than only single-circle perturbations. This can help the optimizer escape local minima that are inaccessible via single moves, leveraging the current program’s strong local search while introducing a mechanism for larger-scale, coordinated improvements.

3. **Adaptive Annealing Schedule Based on Improvement Rate**  
   Replace the fixed exponential temperature decay in simulated annealing with an adaptive schedule that slows cooling when frequent improvements are detected and accelerates it when progress stalls. 4. **Post-Annealing Local Greedy Repacking Sweep**
